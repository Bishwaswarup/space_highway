Phase 3 Summary
===============

What we implemented
- Eigen-analysis of the monodromy matrix to identify stable and unstable directions.
- Manifold generation by perturbing along the eigenvectors (stable/unstable).
- Sampling of manifold trajectories from multiple points along the halo orbit.
- Continuation scheme to generate a family of halo orbits for sampling.
- Serialized outputs for downstream visualization/analysis.

Key files
- `cr3bp/manifolds.py`
- `cr3bp/continuation.py`
- `scripts/phase3_manifolds.py`
- `scripts/phase3_halo_family.py`
- `scripts/phase3_halo_family_pseudo.py`

How to run
`PYTHONPATH=. python3 scripts/phase3_manifolds.py`
`PYTHONPATH=. python3 scripts/phase3_halo_family.py`
`PYTHONPATH=. python3 scripts/phase3_halo_family_pseudo.py`

Outputs
- `outputs/phase3_manifolds.npz`
  - `orbit_times`, `orbit_states`
  - `eigenvalue`, `direction`
  - `manifold_times`, `manifold_trajectories`
- `outputs/phase3_halo_family.npz`
  - `state0s`, `periods`, `z0s`, `vy0s`
- `outputs/phase3_halo_family_pseudo.npz`
  - `state0s`, `periods`, `z0s`, `vy0s`

Notes
- Stable manifolds are generated by reversing time along the stable eigenvector.
